# Canon — THE SIGNAL

## World Rules

1. **The Signal is real.** A low-frequency transmission of unknown origin has been detected in the
   deep ocean. It repeats every 37 minutes. No natural source has been identified.

2. **Memory can be recorded, not transferred.** The Mnemix technology allows a person's episodic
   memories to be encoded as data and stored — but reading another person's memories requires
   direct neural contact. There are no remote uploads.

3. **The Arcadia Institute controls access.** All ocean-floor research in the Pacific Exclusion Zone
   is licensed exclusively through the Arcadia Institute. Their permits cannot be contested.
   Their logs are sealed for 50 years.

4. **Alex Chen is the only one who has heard the full sequence.** The recording equipment fails
   after 2 minutes and 14 seconds. Alex listened without protection and survived — with
   consequences that are still unfolding.
