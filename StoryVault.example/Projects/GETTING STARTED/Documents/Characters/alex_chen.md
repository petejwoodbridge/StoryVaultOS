# Alex Chen — Protagonist

**Age:** 32
**Role:** Marine acoustics researcher, Arcadia Institute
**Status:** On administrative leave (reason: classified)

## Biography
Alex grew up on a container ship off the coast of Fujian, the child of a logistics engineer and
an amateur radio operator. She has been listening to the ocean her entire life — cataloguing whale
song, mapping sonar anomalies, chasing the idea that the deep water is full of language we haven't
learned yet.

She was the lead researcher on Station Seven when the Signal was first detected. She listened to
the full sequence without the prescribed acoustic dampers. The other three researchers in the room
were evacuated after 90 seconds. She stayed for the full 2:14.

## What She Wants
To understand what the Signal is saying — and whether it's addressed to her specifically.

## What She Fears
That the answer will require her to disappear, the same way the three other researchers have
disappeared in the fourteen months since Station Seven was decommissioned.

## Physical
Compact, precise in her movements. Always has a worn notebook in her left pocket and a
waterproof pen clipped to the cover. Rarely makes eye contact — not from shyness but because
she's usually listening to something else.

## Voice
Economical. Says what she means. Hates preamble.
