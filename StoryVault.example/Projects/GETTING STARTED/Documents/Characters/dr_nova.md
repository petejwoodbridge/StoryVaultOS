# Dr. Elias Nova — Antagonist

**Age:** 58
**Role:** Director of Acoustic Research, Arcadia Institute
**Status:** Active

## Biography
Nova built the Arcadia Institute's acoustic division from a single converted navy listening post
into a $2.3 billion research operation. He has published 47 peer-reviewed papers. He has never
been to the ocean floor himself — he manages at a remove, through proxies, through data.

He was the one who approved Station Seven's decommissioning 48 hours after the Signal was
detected. He was the one who classified the recording. He is the one whose personal research
logs Alex suspects contain the full acoustic transcription — the part after 2:14, captured by
a secondary array he's never acknowledged publicly.

## What He Wants
To be the first to decode the Signal and present it to the world on his terms, on his timeline,
with his name attached to the discovery.

## What He Fears
That Alex already understands something he doesn't — that she didn't just hear the Signal but
*received* it — and that the window for controlling that is closing.

## Physical
Tall, grey-haired, immaculate. Speaks slowly, as if he's being recorded. Probably is.

## Voice
Measured, generous-sounding. Always frames control as protection.
