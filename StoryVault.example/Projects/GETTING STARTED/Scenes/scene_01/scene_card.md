# Scene 01 — The Letter

## Location
Alex's apartment. Small, sparse. One wall covered in acoustic spectrograms pinned edge-to-edge.

## Time
Late evening.

## Characters
- Alex Chen

## What happens
Alex receives a registered letter from Arcadia Institute's Legal Division. It is a formal
notification that her research notes — specifically, the handwritten notebook she filled in
the hours after the Station Seven incident — are classified as Institute property and must be
surrendered within 72 hours. She reads it twice. Then she opens the notebook to the last page
she wrote that night. She stares at what's there for a long time. She folds the letter and
puts it in her pocket.

## Scene Function
Inciting incident. Establishes the stakes (the notebook is the only record she controls),
the antagonist force (Arcadia), and Alex's character (she doesn't panic; she plans).

## Emotional Beat
Calm on the surface. Something closing off and hardening underneath.

## Ends on
She picks up her phone. Dials a number. Waits.
