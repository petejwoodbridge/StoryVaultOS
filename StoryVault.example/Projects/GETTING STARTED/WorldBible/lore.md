# World Bible — Lore

## The Signal
First detected on 14 March, 2039, by the autonomous hydrophone array at Station Seven in the
Pacific Exclusion Zone (depth: 4,200m). Frequency: 17.4 Hz — below the threshold of normal
human hearing, but within the range of bone conduction. Duration of recorded sequence: 2
minutes 14 seconds. Repeat interval: exactly 37 minutes. Source: unidentified.

The Signal is not natural. Its harmonic structure does not match any known geological, biological,
or manufactured source. It is, according to three independent acoustic analysts whose reports
were then classified, "structured in a manner consistent with intentional communication."

## Mnemix Technology
Commercial-grade episodic memory encoding became viable in 2031. The process maps a person's
short-term declarative memories onto a protein-lattice storage medium — essentially, a
photograph of recent experience. Reading a Mnemix recording requires physical contact between
the medium and a neural interface. The technology is widely used in medical diagnostics and
legal testimony.

Alex Chen has not encoded her memory of The Signal. She believes — correctly — that a Mnemix
recording of what she heard would be confiscated immediately.

## The Arcadia Institute
Founded 2019, nominally as an independent oceanographic research body. Privately funded.
Seventeen of its twenty-two board members have defense or intelligence backgrounds. The
Pacific Exclusion Zone licence is renewed annually by a treaty arrangement that no signatory
nation has publicly acknowledged.

## The Three Missing Researchers
- **Dr. Park Su-jin** — accepted a position at an unnamed facility in Greenland two weeks after
  Station Seven was decommissioned. Has not published since. Her academic profile was deleted.
- **Tomás Eguia** — listed as "on extended personal leave" by Arcadia. His family says they
  haven't heard from him in nine months.
- **Yuki Brennan** — officially resigned. Alex knows she didn't.
